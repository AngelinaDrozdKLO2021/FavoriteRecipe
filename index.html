No-Bake cheesecake


Ingredients

  • 280 g buiscuits
  • 140 g melted butter
  • 550 ml whipped cream
  • 140 g caster sugar
  • 560 g cream cheese

Directions

  1. Crush buiscuits with a rolling pin until very fine crumbs form
  2. Add butter, and stir until well combined
  3. Press the crumb mixture into a form; press flat. Chill crust in freezer at least 10 minutes
  4. Beat whipped cream and mix with powdered sugar and cream cheese
  5. Put the mixture on top of the cookie crumbs in the cake pan and smooth the top with a spatula or knife.
  6. Refrigerate the cake for at least 2 hours
